DukeEd.com

DukeEd.com was a site that was a fansite of Duke Nukem Forever and it was made by some Unreal Engine 1-based modders to make a community for the upcoming Duke Nukem Forever, but as DNF got delayed more and more, the site became an obscurity as no one visited there, and the owner had to close it in 17th May of 2001, as it was sold to GameSpy (an old popular multiplayer host that got defunct later). DukeEd.com's goal is to give the website the a proper archive of the old one and a new website that has the look and feel of the original one, but has frequent new updates and news of Duke Nukem related projects like the Restoration Project or the DNF Enhanced.
