A rare, but unofficial Doom wad file

This was actually sold in the stores in the 90's in stores and basically most of the sharewares that you had found in stores were not made by id Software except for the game, obviously. The sellers of this wad file did something special and decided to put some sound files from movies (most of them are from mafia genre) and rest of the gameplay remains intact though.
